Welcome to Boys Cross Country

Welcome to Minnetonka Boys Cross Country! We are dedicated to developing student-athletes who excel both on the course and in the classroom.

Our program emphasizes teamwork, dedication, and personal growth. Whether you're a seasoned runner or new to the sport, we welcome you to join our team and be part of our tradition of excellence.

You can follow our Instagram @tonkadistance for the latest updates and team highlights.
